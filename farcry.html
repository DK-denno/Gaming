Far Cry

About game

Far Cry 5 is an action-adventure first-person shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and Ubisoft Toronto
The game is a standalone successor to its predecessor, and takes place in Hope County, a fictional town in Montana.
The main story revolves around doomsday cult the Project at Eden's Gate and its charismatic leader Joseph Seed.
